About

Prodigy Infotech offers a comprehensive internship program in web development, designed to provide participants with hands-on experience and practical knowledge in the field. The program focuses on key areas such as front-end and back-end development, utilizing technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks such as React or Angular for front-end, and Node.js or Django for back-end. Interns have the opportunity to work on real-world projects, enhancing their coding skills, problem-solving abilities, and understanding of web architecture. Guided by experienced mentors, interns receive valuable feedback and industry insights, preparing them for successful careers in web development. The collaborative and innovative environment at Prodigy Infotech fosters professional growth and networking, making it an ideal setting for aspiring web developers.
